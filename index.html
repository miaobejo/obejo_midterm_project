Welcome to Barangay Bacag!

Bacag is a barangay in the municipality of Villasis, in the province of Pangasinan. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 6,093. This represented 9.37% of the total population of Villasis.

WELCOME

    Welcome to Barangay Bacag's digital system, designed to streamline the process of obtaining a Barangay Clearance in the Philippines. This essential document serves as proof of identity, residency, and good behavior within our community. Online appointment scheduling for Barangay Clearance issuance is now available for Bacag residents, providing a convenient and efficient way to access this service.

    Residents can easily schedule appointments from the comfort of their homes or mobile devices, reducing the need for long queues at the Barangay office. This online system helps manage the flow of residents, ensuring an organized and streamlined process. This, in turn, leads to quicker service and less administrative burden on the Barangay office, particularly during times of public health concerns, where minimizing physical contact is crucial.

Here are the steps to book an appointment:

Step 1: Access the website and read the instructions on how to book online.
Step 2: Select your preferred appointment services.
Step 3: Fill out the required personal and service information, then click the SAVE button to confirm your appointment.
Step 4: After saving, a notification will appear, and it's important to screenshot it for your appointment code reference.

Thank you for using our online appointment system. We aim to make the Barangay Clearance issuance process efficient and accessible for all Bacag residents.

Streets


Here are the streets of barangay bacag

Barangay Bacag beckons with allure, boasting a delightful ensemble of four enchanting streets—Bacag North, Bacag West, Bacag East, and
Bacag Sur—inviting residents to explore a tapestry of charm where each avenue unfolds a unique story, weaving together the
vibrant essence of a truly captivating community.


Bacag Sur

Bacag Sur, nestled along the highway, is a bustling hub featuring diverse businesses like Citihardware, Wilcon, La Corona, Alibaba, and Purina.


Bacag East

Bacag East, anchored by the notable landmark IPI, is a central point where the street are, adding to its accessibility and significance in the area.

Bacag West

Bacag West is easily found with a visible sign, and landmarks like the Golden Piggery Farm make it stand out in the area.

Bacag North

Bacag North serves as the counterpart to the Bacag Barangay Hall and Bacag Elementary School, forming a cohesive cluster in the area.
